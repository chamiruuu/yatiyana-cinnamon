About Us

Nature is the lifeline of our business. We believe in respecting and giving back to nature at every step of our operational process.

Our Sustainable Practices

From sourcing ingredients to labeling products, we follow sustainable practices that benefit the planet. Our production plants are structured to reduce waste and use energy efficiently.

For example, we collect rainwater for cooling tanks and reuse water in our production process to conserve resources.

Additionally, our boilers utilize steam-extracted cinnamon leaves to generate energy, further minimizing waste.

Partnerships for Sustainability

We plan to streamline operations to reduce our carbon footprint, conserve biodiversity, and manage waste systematically by fostering strong partnerships.

Community Contributions

Yatiyana Cinnamon contributes donations to a nearby school, reinforcing our commitment to sustainable growth and community support.
