Alba

Ceylon Cinnamon Sticks, specifically the Alba grade, are the thinnest and finest quality within the cinnamon bark (stick) group, making them the most expensive grade available. These sticks have a diameter ranging from 8 mm to 10 mm, similar to that of a pencil. Despite their size, Alba cinnamon offers an exceptional taste and a fresh, exotic aroma, distinguishing it from other grades. This top-tier quality is the best Ceylon cinnamon you can purchase from Sri Lanka. Due to its superior taste, Alba cinnamon is highly sought after and rare in the global cinnamon market.

Grade C5

Our premium quality C5 grade 5-inch Ceylon Cinnamon sticks feature ultra-low coumarin levels, an exotic aroma, and are non-irradiated and salt-free. Sealed in our new triple-layer stand-up pouch to protect from UV damage and preserve freshness, these high-quality cinnamon sticks meet export quality standards and are selected for their colour, shape, small size, quilling density, foxing percentage, fresh taste, and aroma. Due to the mild nature of Ceylon Cinnamon, you may not notice an immediate aroma when opening the pouch. However, breaking a stick in half will release its refined aroma, distinct from the pungent cassia-type cinnamon. Boiling the sticks into tea further enhances their aroma and taste.

Grade C4

The C4 Cinnamon grade is one of the finest Ceylon Cinnamon grades, ranking just below the C5 grade in terms of thickness and price. This special Continental grade has a diameter of 15 cm and is sourced from the southern part of Sri Lanka, known for producing the best true cinnamon. Unlike regular Cassia Cinnamon, Ceylon Cinnamon sticks are soft, rolled like cigars with multiple thin strips of bark, and easily flake for grinding into fine powder, perfect for adding to curries, rice, or custards. Like the Alba grade, C4 grade Ceylon cinnamon is highly regarded for export and is ideal for use in sweet dishes, desserts, and as sprinkles in lattes or hot chocolate in its powdered form.

Grade H1

The H1 Cinnamon grade, part of the Ceylon Cinnamon grades, is the best cinnamon of the Hamburg (H) grade, with the lowest quill diameter of 23-24 mm. It features a pale brown hue, distinct flavour and scent, and a coarse texture. Ranking just below the C4 Cinnamon in thickness and price, this Real Ceylon Cinnamon grade is a special Continental variety with a diameter of 18-22 mm.

Grade H2

The H2 Cinnamon grade is one of the Ceylon Cinnamon grades, ranking just below the H1 Cinnamon in thickness and price. This Real Ceylon Cinnamon grade is a special Continental variety with a diameter of 25-27 mm.

Cinnamon Bark Oil

Cinnamon bark oil, extracted from trees like Cinnamomum verum (Ceylon cinnamon), contains key compounds such as cinnamaldehyde, cinnamyl acetate, eugenol, and eugenol acetate, lending it a characteristic warming scent. Widely utilised in aromatherapy and as a flavouring agent, its concentrated nature requires careful use to avoid overpowering dishes. Known for its stress-reducing properties and ability to enhance mood, the aroma of cinnamon bark oil creates a warm and inviting atmosphere in various settings.
