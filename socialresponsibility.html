Sustainability at the Heart of Our Business

Nature is the lifeline of our business. That is why we believe in respecting and giving back to nature at every step of our operational process. From sourcing ingredients to labelling the products, we follow a range of sustainable practices that benefit the planet in the long run.

Energy Efficiency

Additionally, once the cinnamon leaves are steamed to extract oil, the used leaves are fed into our boilers to generate energy. The water used in our plants to produce cinnamon oil is stored in separate tanks and reused in production, ensuring it is not released into the environment.

Waste Management

As we advance, we aim to streamline operations to lower our carbon footprint, conserve biodiversity, and manage waste systematically through robust partnerships. This proactive approach underscores our commitment to sustainable practices and environmental stewardship.

Sourcing Ingredients

Our production plants are meticulously designed to minimize waste and optimize energy efficiency. Rainwater is collected for cooling tanks, conserving precious tap water for drinking purposes. This sustainable practice exemplifies our commitment to resource conservation and responsible environmental stewardship.

Environmental Conservation

Environmental conservation involves various practices aimed at preserving and protecting our planet. This includes efforts to reduce carbon footprints, conserve biodiversity, and manage waste systematically. These initiatives are crucial for ensuring sustainable and responsible stewardship of our natural resources for future generations.
