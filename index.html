SCENTED HERITAGE

True cinnamon, native to Sri Lanka, has been prized for millennia, valued in ancient Egypt and the bible. European powers vied for its trade, but Sri Lanka remains the top producer, harvesting it by drying the inner bark into quills. Renowned for its flavor, aroma, and medicinal properties, true cinnamon is also cultivated in India, Madagascar, and the Seychelles.

SUSTAINABILITY
AT OUR CORE

At yatiyana cinnamon, nature is the cornerstone of our business. We implement sustainable practices at every step, from sourcing ingredients to labelling products, ensuring long-term benefits for the planet. Our production plants are designed to reduce waste and use energy efficiently, including rainwater collection and reusing steam-processed cinnamon leaves for energy.
