Our Commitment to Sustainability

Nature is the lifeline of our business. That is why we believe in respecting and giving back to nature at every step of our operational process. From sourcing ingredients to labelling the products, we follow a range of sustainable practices that benefit the planet in the long run.

Sourcing Ingredients

Our production plants are structured to reduce waste and use energy efficiently. For example, we collect rainwater for our cooling tanks to avoid wasting tap water, which can be used for drinking purposes.

Energy Efficiency

Additionally, once the cinnamon leaves are steamed to extract oil, the used leaves are fed into our boilers to generate energy. The water used in our plants to produce cinnamon oil is stored in separate tanks and reused in production, ensuring it is not released into the environment.

Waste Management

As we progress in our business, we plan to streamline our operations to reduce our carbon footprint, conserve biodiversity, and manage waste systematically by fostering strong partnerships.

Environmental Conservation

Yatiyana Cinnamon is committed to sustainable growth. Therefore, the company contributes many donations to a school situated near our Alpitiya factory.
