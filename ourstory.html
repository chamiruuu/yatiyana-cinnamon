our story

Yatiyana Cinnamon combines ancient wisdom, natural bio-diverse estates, and the highest quality standards to bring its valued clientele one of our tropical island’s most cherished ingredients – cinnamon. We believe that the true benefits and uses of Ceylon cinnamon in all its forms are not yet fully understood or enjoyed by most of modern society. While once considered a jewel in the markets of the ancient world, there is much more that this miraculous product can offer to enrich wellness and life – and we are determined to make a difference. Yatiyana Cinnamon began in 2021 when we established roots in Sri Lanka’s vibrant agricultural industry. Our humble beginnings centred around two estates in the south of the nation, historically the province where the finest and most luxurious Ceylon products were traded with kingdoms across the world. Today, thanks to Sri Lanka’s incomparable natural resources and our team of small-scale growers and harvesters in the local community, we protect and deliver the herbal benefits of our organic cinnamon product range to the world.
